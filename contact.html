When is the best time to visit Tanzania?

The dry season (June to October) is ideal for safaris, while December to March offers great weather and calving season in Serengeti. We can help you choose the perfect time based on your interests.

What's included in your tour packages?

Our packages typically include accommodation, meals, transportation, park fees, professional guides, and activities. We provide detailed itineraries with all inclusions clearly listed.

Do I need experience to climb Kilimanjaro?

No previous climbing experience is required, but good physical fitness is essential. We offer different routes to match various fitness levels and provide comprehensive preparation guidance.

Is Tanzania safe for tourists?

Tanzania is generally very safe for tourists, especially in safari areas and with professional guides. We prioritize your safety and provide 24/7 support during your travels.

What vaccinations do I need?

Yellow fever vaccination is required if coming from infected areas. We recommend consulting your doctor about malaria prophylaxis and other recommended vaccinations 4-6 weeks before travel.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance, especially for peak seasons (June-October, December-March). However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.
