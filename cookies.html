1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit websites. They contain information about your browsing activity and preferences, helping websites remember you and provide a more personalized experience.

Cookies serve many purposes, from enabling basic website functionality to providing insights for website improvements. They are widely used by almost all websites to enhance user experience and provide valuable analytics to website operators.

Did You Know?

Cookies cannot harm your device or access personal files. They are simply small pieces of data that help websites work better for you.

How Cookies Work

When you visit Nova Trails' website, we may place cookies on your device. These cookies allow us to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Provide relevant content and advertisements
  • Measure website performance and effectiveness

5. Cookie Duration

Cookies have different lifespans depending on their purpose and type:

Session Cookies

These temporary cookies are deleted when you close your browser. They're used for essential functions like maintaining your session while browsing our website.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They remember your preferences and settings for future visits. Our persistent cookies typically last:

  • Preference cookies: 1-2 years
  • Analytics cookies: 2 years
  • Marketing cookies: 30 days to 2 years
  • Functional cookies: 30 days to 1 year

6. Browser Cookie Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

🌐
Google Chrome
Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data → Customize preferences
🦊
Mozilla Firefox
Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Manage settings
🧭
Safari
Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data → Manage website data
🔷
Microsoft Edge
Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data → Manage

Important Note

Please note that completely blocking cookies may prevent certain parts of our website from functioning properly. Some features may become unavailable or not work as intended.

7. Mobile Device Controls

Mobile devices have their own privacy controls for tracking and cookies:

iOS Devices (iPhone/iPad)

  • Settings → Safari → Privacy & Security → Block All Cookies
  • Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads
  • Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements

Android Devices

  • Chrome: Settings → Site Settings → Cookies
  • Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Settings → Privacy → Advanced → Website Settings

8. Consequences of Disabling Cookies

While you have the right to disable cookies, doing so may affect your experience on our website:

If You Disable Essential Cookies

  • The website may not function properly
  • You may not be able to submit booking inquiries
  • Session data may be lost when navigating pages
  • Security features may not work correctly

If You Disable Functional Cookies

  • Your preferences won't be remembered
  • You'll need to re-enter information on each visit
  • The website experience won't be personalized
  • Language and region settings won't be saved

If You Disable Performance Cookies

  • We can't improve our website based on user behavior
  • Technical issues may take longer to identify and fix
  • Website optimization will be less effective

If You Disable Marketing Cookies

  • Advertisements may be less relevant to your interests
  • You may see the same ads repeatedly
  • We can't measure marketing campaign effectiveness
  • Retargeting campaigns won't work properly
