Our Story Begins in the Heart of Africa

Nova Trails was born from a simple belief: that the world's most extraordinary experiences happen when you venture beyond the ordinary. Founded in 2018 by a team of passionate explorers who fell in love with Tanzania's untamed beauty, we've dedicated ourselves to sharing these incredible landscapes with fellow adventurers.

What started as a small group of friends sharing their love for the Serengeti has grown into Tanzania's premier adventure travel company. Yet our mission remains unchanged: to create authentic, transformative experiences that connect travelers with the raw beauty of East Africa while supporting local communities and conservation efforts.

Every journey we craft tells a story ‚Äì of ancient migrations, towering peaks, pristine beaches, and the warm-hearted people who call this land home. We're not just tour operators; we're storytellers, conservationists, and your gateway to experiences that will change how you see the world.
