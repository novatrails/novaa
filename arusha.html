Safari Capital of the World

The Arusha Region is the undisputed safari capital of Tanzania, serving as the gateway to some of Africa's most iconic wildlife destinations. This region houses the legendary Serengeti National Park, the spectacular Ngorongoro Crater, and Tarangire National Park.

From the Great Migration that sweeps across the Serengeti plains to the concentrated wildlife viewing in the Ngorongoro Crater, the Arusha Region offers unparalleled safari experiences that have captivated travelers for generations.

Experience close encounters with the Big Five, witness nature's greatest spectacle, and create memories that will last a lifetime in this remarkable wilderness.

Iconic Wildlife

Meet the incredible animals that call the Arusha Region home

🦁

African Lions

Experience the thrill of encountering the king of beasts in their natural habitat, from the pride dynamics to spectacular hunting displays.

🐘

African Elephants

Witness these gentle giants in massive herds, especially in Tarangire, where you can observe their complex social structures and behaviors.

🦏

Black Rhinoceros

Spot the critically endangered black rhino in Ngorongoro Crater, one of the best places in Africa for rhino sightings.

🐆

Leopards

The elusive spotted cats that master the art of camouflage, often spotted resting in acacia trees during the day.

🦌

Great Migration

Witness over 2 million wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles in their annual journey across the Serengeti ecosystem.

🦒

Giraffes

The tallest mammals on Earth, gracefully browsing acacia trees and providing some of the most photogenic safari moments.

Best Time to Visit

Plan your safari for the perfect wildlife viewing experience

🌞

Dry Season

June - October

Peak safari season with excellent wildlife viewing as animals gather around water sources. Clear skies and minimal rainfall make for comfortable game drives.

🌧️

Short Rains

November - December

Fresh green landscapes with fewer crowds. Great for bird watching as migratory species arrive. The weather is generally pleasant with short afternoon showers.

🌿

Calving Season

January - March

Wildebeest calving season in the southern Serengeti. Witness new life and predator action. Lush green scenery with occasional short rains.

🌊

Long Rains

April - May

Lower prices and dramatic landscapes. Some roads may be challenging, but wildlife viewing remains excellent with lush vegetation and active animal behavior.
