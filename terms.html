6. Travel Requirements

Documentation

All travelers must ensure they have valid travel documentation:

  • Passport valid for at least 6 months from travel date
  • Appropriate visa for Tanzania (if required)
  • Yellow fever vaccination certificate (if arriving from endemic areas)
  • Travel insurance documentation
  • Medical certificates (if required for specific activities)

Health and Fitness

Certain activities require adequate physical fitness and good health:

  • Mount Kilimanjaro treks require excellent physical condition
  • Safari activities involve long drives on rough terrain
  • Some accommodations may not be suitable for mobility-impaired guests
  • Medical facilities may be limited in remote areas

Age Restrictions

Certain activities have age restrictions for safety reasons:

  • Kilimanjaro treks: Minimum age 12 years
  • Some walking safaris: Minimum age 16 years
  • Children under 18 must be accompanied by a guardian
  • Special arrangements may be available for families

Prohibited Items

The following items are prohibited during our tours:

  • Illegal drugs and controlled substances
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Items that may disturb wildlife
  • Excessive amounts of alcohol
  • Drones (without proper permits)
